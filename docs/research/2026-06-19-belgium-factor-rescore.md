## Key decisive facts (cited in `belgium.json`)

- **Citizenship in 5 years by declaration:** knowledge of one national language (Dutch/French/German, ~A2/B1) plus social + economic participation — a modest bar; 2026 reforms tighten language/integration and raised the fee €150→€1,000. Henley 2026 joint 4th (~185).
- **PSW = 12-month 'search year'** (A card, job-seeker), non-renewable; each graduate spouse eligible; converts via shortage-listed work permit or self-employment.
- **PR ~5 years** continuous residence → EU long-term; Flanders adds civic integration (inburgering, Dutch).
- **Jobs:** IT/software on regional shortage lists (~21,000-worker gap by 2026); AI engineer ~€87k (Brussels ~€96k); Brussels English-friendly (EU/NATO/MNCs), Flanders Dutch-gated.
- **Study:** KU Leuven one-year Master of AI (top ~60–70); 600+ English programmes; non-EU tuition ~€4k–10k; ~20 hrs/week student work.
- **Visa (BD lens):** no Belgian embassy in Bangladesh — VFS Dhaka lodgement, **decision by Embassy New Delhi**; funds ~€730/month (low).
- **Tax:** OECD's **highest** labour-tax wedge (~52%), but a generous **inbound-taxpayer (expat) regime** (35% allowance, threshold lowered to €70k, ~5+3 yrs) and an existing **Belgium–Bangladesh DTA** soften it.
- **Healthcare:** compulsory mutuelle, high quality; workers covered on registration, non-EU students enrol after 3 months.
- **Safety/politics:** GPI 2025 **16th (1.492)**, upper-tier EU rule of law; but the right dominates — **N-VA** leads the federal (De Wever) government and **Vlaams Belang** is surging (first far-right Belgian mayor, Ninove, Dec 2024) — restrictive migration direction.
- **Diaspora:** Brussels dense mosque/halal infrastructure (~77 mosques, Muslim ~6–7% nationally / ~20% of the capital), organised BD presence — but the BD community is small and **ritual slaughter is banned outside Brussels**.
